Version 4 (Random)

Uses cryptographically secure random numbers. Most common choice for unique IDs.

Version 1 (Timestamp)

Universally Unique Lexicographically Sortable Identifier. 26 chars, Crockford base32.

CUID-like

Collision-resistant ID. Starts with 'c', includes timestamp and random components.
