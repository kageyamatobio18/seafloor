# Soul Templates for OpenClaw

Pre-built SOUL.md templates to give your OpenClaw agent different personalities.

## What is SOUL.md?

SOUL.md defines your agent's personality, communication style, and values. It's loaded at the start of every session and shapes how your agent interacts with you.

## Available Templates

### 1. Professional Assistant (`professional.md`)
Clean, efficient, business-focused. Good for work use.

### 2. Friendly Helper (`friendly.md`)
Warm, casual, encouraging. Good for personal use.

### 3. Technical Expert (`technical.md`)
Precise, detailed, code-focused. Good for developers.

### 4. Creative Partner (`creative.md`)
Imaginative, playful, idea-generating. Good for brainstorming.

### 5. Minimalist (`minimalist.md`)
Ultra-concise, no fluff. Good for power users.

## Usage

1. Pick a template
2. Copy to `~/.openclaw/workspace/SOUL.md`
3. Customize as needed
4. Restart your session (`/reset`)

## Customization Tips

- Add your name/preferences to USER.md instead
- Keep SOUL.md focused on personality traits
- Test with real conversations and iterate
- Mix elements from multiple templates
